Video Projects

Below is a showcase of the diverse range of videos I’ve created, spanning from lyric videos to fully animated content. Each project reflects a unique blend of creativity and technical skill, from transforming static artwork into engaging lyric animations to producing intricate motion graphics that bring concepts to life. My work includes promotional videos, brand-aligned animations, and tailored visual content designed to enhance audience engagement and convey clear, compelling messages across various platforms.


Music Lyric Video for ‘Just Kiddin’




I was commissioned to create animated lyrics for the DJ duo Just Kiddin, working with their original artwork. I animated the existing design, adding new elements that seamlessly complemented and matched the original style. Additionally, I sourced the original typography to ensure the animated lyrics aligned perfectly with the visual aesthetic.


A&M Productions Exhibition Promotional Video




I collaborated with A&M Productions to create a video showcased at their exhibition stand during the ABTT Theatre Show, an event that brings together the largest gathering of specialist suppliers in live performance technology, production, and education. The video was designed to highlight the company's unique selling points and attract attendees from the theatre, performance, events, festivals, and music industries.


Lewis Thompson Animated Artwork Visuals for Concert Performances




I animated the original artwork from the artist's latest singles, transforming them into visually engaging loops with a strong focus on highlighting the artist’s name. I also created variations of the original artwork to enhance the visual appeal.



These animations were showcased during Lewis’s live performance at the Life is Beautiful festival in Las Vegas, Nevada, a three-day event that draws over 170,000 attendees.


Elm Eco-Banner Videos




I filmed and produced a lighthearted, tongue-in-cheek video that effectively balanced humour with clear, informative content. To demonstrate how quickly the banner could be assembled, the team participated in a timed challenge, adding an engaging and interactive element. The approach successfully captured user interest while delivering key information in an entertaining way.


Practical Action Christmas Video




I created a charity video to express gratitude to donors during the Christmas season. The video was well-received throughout the organisation, leading to the creation of a Spanish version at the request of the charity's offices in Peru and Bolivia.


Fuster & Associates Facebook Targeted Video Campaign Plusvalia Tax




The video provided a clear explanation of how to reclaim the Plusvalia Tax, serving as a key component of a broader campaign designed to inform and educate users about this issue.



Through retargeting Facebook adverts, the campaign specifically reached those affected, directing them to a free infographic guide that I created to offer further assistance and insights.


Groove On Live Event Promotional Video




I created a promotional video for the Groove On event, using the radio advert's audio as the foundation. Alongside developing the event’s brand identity, I managed the design and production of all digital and printed posters, as well as ticket designs. The video was carefully crafted to align with the other promotional materials, ensuring consistent and cohesive branding across all platforms.
